# Problem Statement

## Product

RuleForge - governance-first KYT template and threshold management platform for VASPs.

## Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

- Compliance leads and MLROs at VASPs
- Monitoring operations managers responsible for alert quality and throughput
- Teams using Chainalysis, TRM, Crystal, Fireblocks, or mixed-provider stacks

## Core Problem

Teams can buy analytics signals, but they still struggle to run consistent, auditable policy decisions on top of those signals.

Common failure modes:

1. Thresholds are manually edited without durable rationale.
2. Policy intent drifts from operational execution over time.
3. Changes are deployed without simulation or rollback discipline.
4. Audit requests trigger manual evidence reconstruction.

## Jobs To Be Done (JTBD)

- "When typologies or regulator expectations change, help us update thresholds quickly without losing audit defensibility."
- "When alert volumes spike, help us reduce noise without weakening detection."
- "When stakeholders challenge a decision, help us show clear rationale, ownership, and change history."

## Why Current Alternatives Fail

- Provider-native setups are signal-rich but governance-light.
- Spreadsheets are flexible but non-governed and hard to audit.
- Pure advisory outputs are hard to operationalize repeatedly.

## Product Promise

RuleForge provides an implementation layer where policy logic, threshold changes, evidence, and simulation can be managed as one workflow.
