# Roadmap - Now / Next / Later

## Prioritization Method

Scoring lens:

- User pain reduction
- Compliance risk reduction
- Revenue impact
- Implementation complexity

Priority favors features that reduce operational risk and improve trust-to-purchase conversion.

## Now (0-30 days)

1. Conversion trust upgrades on landing page (proof chain, legal clarity, value framing).
2. Production readiness fixes (broken paths/imports, consistent brand and contact handling).
3. Product operating artifacts (metrics, roadmap, decision log, experiment log).

Success criteria:

- Stable production build
- Reduced messaging inconsistency
- Clear PM evidence for external reviewers

## Next (30-90 days)

1. Baseline analytics instrumentation (funnel and activation events).
2. Lightweight ROI estimator for compliance teams.
3. Change-control workflow templates with downloadable implementation pack.
4. Enterprise lead qualification flow (structured intake form + routing).

Success criteria:

- Visibility into conversion bottlenecks
- Better enterprise lead quality
- Faster buyer education cycle

## Later (90+ days)

1. Adapter framework examples for two providers (prove vendor-agnostic portability).
2. Scenario simulation dashboard for threshold impact previews.
3. Team-level governance permissions and approval workflow.
4. AI-assisted policy drafting assistant with mandatory human approval gates.

Success criteria:

- Stronger defensibility moat
- Higher switching cost through governance workflows
- Clear "AI-native but human-governed" product identity
